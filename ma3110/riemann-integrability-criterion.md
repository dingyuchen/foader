# Riemann Integrability Criterion

Let $f : [a, b] \rightarrow \R$ be a bounded function.

Then $f$ is integrable on $[a, b]$ iff for any $\epsilon > 0$, there exists a partition $P$ of $[a, b]$ such that

$$U(f, P) - L(f, P) < \epsilon$$
